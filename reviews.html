References

Need more confidence before booking?

If a homeowner wants extra reassurance, invite them to call and ask about the type of project they are planning. That makes it easier to offer the most relevant reference or example of similar work.

Best questions to ask

  • Have you completed similar work nearby?
  • What is the likely sequence of the job?
  • How do you manage communication during the project?
  • What information should we prepare before a site visit?
